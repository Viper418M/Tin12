MY DREAM JOB: ENGLISH TEACHER

  1. Key Responsibilities

    • Plan and deliver engaging English lessons aligned with curriculum standards
    • Teach language skills (grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation) and/or literature
    • Assess students’ progress through tests, assignments, and classroom activities
    • Provide feedback to improve students’ language proficiency
    • Create a positive and inclusive learning environment
    • Adapt teaching methods to suit different learning levels and styles

  2. Work Settings

    • Primary, secondary, or high schools
    • Language centers and private institutes
    • Colleges and universities
    • Đường truyền hữu tuyến và vô tuyến
    • Online teaching platforms
